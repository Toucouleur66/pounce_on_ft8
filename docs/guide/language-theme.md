# Language & Theme

## Language

Wait and Pounce is fully translated. Choose your language from the **Language** menu:

- **English**
- **Français**
- **中文** (Chinese)
- **日本語** (Japanese)
- **Українська** (Ukrainian)

::: tip Restart required
Changing language takes effect after a **restart** of the application. The app will prompt you.
:::

Everything is translated — menus, settings, tooltips and the grid map are all localised.

## Theme

Choose the colour scheme from **View → Theme**:

| Theme | Behaviour |
|---|---|
| **Light** | Always light. |
| **Dark** | Always dark. |
| **System** | Follow the OS appearance (default). |

The theme applies live to the whole UI, including the decodes table colours and the status bar.
On macOS the window chrome is matched to the chosen appearance.

## View options

The **View** menu also offers display tweaks that pair well with theming:

- **Compact View** (<kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>C</kbd>) and **Alternate View** — denser layouts.
- **Always on Top** — keep the window above WSJT-X.
- **Show All Messages** (<kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>A</kbd>) — show every decode, not just matches.
- **Format Time** submenu — show absolute **Time** or relative **Age** in the table.

See the full list in [Keyboard Shortcuts](/reference/shortcuts).
